# Privacy Policy for Kedi

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy is paramount. Kedi is built from the ground up to respect your data and your source code.

## 1. Local-First Processing & Security
Kedi is a standalone application that runs on your local machine. It reads your local repository data directly using native macOS components. Your source code and credentials remain secure on your Mac and are never transmitted to any third-party app servers.

## 2. Git Server Connections
Kedi connects exclusively to your designated Git servers (such as GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, or your own self-hosted servers) to perform standard Git operations like fetching updates, viewing diffs, and syncing code. These connections are made directly from your machine to your Git provider using your local SSH keys or personal access tokens.

## 3. No Telemetry & No Backdoors
* **Zero Telemetry:** Kedi does not collect, track, or log any analytics, usage metrics, or personal data. There are no background tracking scripts or third-party marketing SDKs included in the app.
* **No Backdoors:** There are absolutely no hidden backdoors, unauthorized network transmissions, or external tracking mechanisms.
